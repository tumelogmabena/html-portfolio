The Best Series To Watch

By Tumelo G Mabena

1. How To Get Away With Murder

This was one great show that kept me glued onto the screen for several weeks, withou any loss in interest or bordom . HTGAWM is about the accidental murder of a lecturers husband by a group of students and the lecturer helping them get away with it. From the first episode you're hooked, I believe it was well written and directed. Tried to make it my Christamas tradition to watch every year, lol.

Rating: 5/5

Completion: 4/5

2. Suits

Suits is a show where one could genuinely be exposed to my commitment when it comes to watching shows. I think I have been battling with this watch for almost 2 years now. Its a really great watch dont get me wrong but when a show of 45 mins per episode, and has 16 episodes per season and has round about 9 seasons, I bet anyone will have problems commiting till the end. This show is about a false lawyer who is a great lawyer, who works under the greates law firm under the greates lawyer in town. As they fight for their clients they are also fighting to not get caught in thier little act.

Rating: 5/5

Completion: 3/5

3. The 100

The 100, was such a great watch, puting it 3rd on the list is for technical reasons. The show itself is great but my personal opinion is there isn't much of evolution both in the characters and the story line, yes the show is primarily about survival, but pulling in aspects other than survival wouldnt a bad thing at all. The ending as well didnt sit well with me, but hey I finished the show, I hardly do that.

Rating: 4/5

Completion: 4/5
